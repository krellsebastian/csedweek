Installation of PocketCode

There are two possibilities how to install PocketCode:

  • From PC
  • From Smartphone

Step 1: If you are right now sitting in front of your PC click on the following link: Let's go.

Step 2: Click on 'Install'. If you are not logged in, sign in in with your Google account. (Hint: Use the same account with which you are logged in on your smartphone or tablet)

Step 3: The app will now be installed on your smartphone automatically. (Hint: Your smartphone needs a connection to the internet in order to install the app)

Step 1: Open your playstore-app.

Step 2: Enter 'PocketCode' in the search bar and choose the first result. Then, click 'Install'.

Step 3: Accept the app conditions. PocketCode will now be installed.
